Invitation!

Dear colleagues,

We are pleased to announce to you that we have started the congress work with great excitement with our organization committee for Congress on Education, Research and Innovation Congress in Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation, which will be held at Izmir Katip Çelebi University between 9-11 May 2024.

We plan to include the topics of Education and Research in our congress that the main theme is Innovation in Physiotherapy and Rehabilitation. We work to organize the scientific program and organization. We will share the details about our congress with you on our website in the future.

We invite you, our valuable participants, to attend our congress in the peaceful days of spring and the city in Izmir which is the pearl of the Aegean, and we look forward to seeing you among us.

On behalf of the Congress Organizing Committee,

Prof. Dr. Derya ÖZER KAYA
